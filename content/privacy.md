# Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

## Data Collection

This website uses Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors engage with our content. The following data is collected:

- Pages visited
- Time spent on pages
- Referrer (how you found us)
- Device/browser type
- Approximate location (country/region)

## Cookies

We use a single cookie to remember your privacy preferences:

- `bgslabs_consent`: Stores your choice (accept/reject) for analytics

## Third-Party Services

- **Google Analytics 4**: Visitor statistics
- **GitHub Pages**: Hosting infrastructure

## Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

- Request access to your data (we don't store personal data)
- Request deletion of your data (clear your browser localStorage)
- Opt-out of tracking (use the "Reject" button in our cookie banner)
