ZMM31 (512 bits) = 8 lanes of 64 bits each
    |-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
    |  0  |  1  |  2  |  3  |  4  |  5  |  6  |  7  |

YMM15 (256 bits) = 4 lanes of 64 bits each
    |-------|-------|-------|-------|
    |   0   |   1   |   2   |   3   |

XMM0  (128 bits) = 2 lanes of 64 bits each  
    |-----------|-----------|
    |     0     |     1     |
